The power of AI will lie in the speed in which it can analyze vast amounts
of data and suggest design adjustments. A designer can then cherry-
pick and approve adjustments based on that data to help users
understand more about our products.

Augmented reality (AR) is an experience where designers enhance parts
of users’ physical world with computer-generated input. Designers can
create in digital content which responds in real time to changes in the
user’s environment, typically movement.

Armis aims to make this possible for our users in our products to get the
ultimate experince of AR in your hands.
